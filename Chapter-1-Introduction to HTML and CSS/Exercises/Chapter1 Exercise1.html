Mozzarella Sticks



Mozzarella Sticks are a delicious quick and easy-to-make snack that make for great appetizers! The recipe itself is fairly straightforward and can be a fun way to introduce children to cooking, as they make a great activity alongside adults.


Ingredients

For the batter, you will need:

  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Flour

For the cheese, you will need:

  • Mozzarella Cheese Sticks

Method

  1. Prepare three bowls for the breading of the sticks.
  2. Place the eggs, flour, and breadcrumbs in those three seperate bowls.
  3. With your left hand, coat the mozzarella stick in the flour.
  4. With your right hand, coat the mozarella stick in the eggs.
  5. Then again, with your left hand, coat the mozarella sticks in the breadcrumbs.
  6. Repeat this process two times to secure the cheese within the breading.
  7. Place each stick on a plate, and keep in freezer for 30 minutes, up to an hour.
  8. Pour oil into a deep saucepan, enough to cover the sticks.
  9. Fry sticks until golden brown.
  10. Drain the oil from the sticks after frying by placing atop paper towels.
  11. Serve alongside dipping sauce of choice.
